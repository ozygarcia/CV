Military Service

I served in the United States Marine Corps for 8 1/2 years. I reached the rank of Sergeant during this time.
I was stationed in Okinawa, Saudi Arabia, and Bahrain during my years of service. I took part in Operation
Desert Storm/ Desert Shield.

During my enlistment I was trained in the following:

  • Field Radio Operator
  • Microwave Operator
  • Tactical Satellite Operator.
